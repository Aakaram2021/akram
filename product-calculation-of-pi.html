Calculation of Pi - an Intuitive Way to Understand Pi

  • ₹ 299

Calculation of Pi- An intuitive way to understand Pi A Math Model to calculate the Pi up to Two decimals accuracy. This product is a set of three different circles, three scales of Diameter. The thread is used to calculate the value PI practically. It is helpful to teachers, students, and parents to understand the value of PI. It can be part of the MATH LAB of any school and college. Process Step 1 - Calculate Perimeter of Circle : A. Fit the given thread in the screw on Pi Board. B. Round it on the perimeter of the circle and complete a circle. C. cut the thread. Step 2 - Use the diameter scale which divided into 100 equal parts and measure the length of the perimeter. Observation: Length should be 314 parts on the scale. Or Step 2 - Divide the Perimeter thread in the length of Diameter using a diameter scale. Observation: thread is divided into three parts and the remaining small part of the thread is of length 14 parts as per the Diameter scale.
